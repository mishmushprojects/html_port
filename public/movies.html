The Best Movies According to Masha

My Top 4 Movies of All Time

Spirited Away

It is a beautiful film, even my Granny loved it.

Buck

A movie with the original "Horse Whisperer" Buck Brannaman. A beautiful film really showing the power of understanding.
Violence is not the solution. This film was likely my first example of nonviolent communication.

How to Train Your Dragon

This First film is beautiful. I still remember watching it in a small discount movie theater in London. I remember walking out and living in a world of Fantasy.

Tibet: The Cry of the Snow Lion

This is a beautiful introduction to Tibet, buddhism, compassionate understanding. I wanted to know how to see the world as the Monks see the world, and eventually I found this.
